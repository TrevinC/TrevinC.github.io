I am an undergraduate student at Illinois Tech pursuing a degree in Aerospace engineering with a minor in Astrophysics. I will be graduating this spring year and am currently applying for PhD programs for the fall. I plan to continue my research and look forward to integrating my engineering skills into future astrophysics projects. The advances of the JPL and NASA research teams have been the driving inspiration in my pursuit of a career in research. I believe that space travel will be able to solve some of the core questions the universe is offering. The advances we've made in understanding physics terrestrially are expansive, but pairing that with the ability to go beyond our atmosphere will be the next step. Asteroids and exoplanets are not resource troves to be exploited but frontiers to be explored that will help bridge the gaps in our understanding.
