Exoplanet Research

Welcome to my exoplanet research page. Below you'll find links to important exoplanet research resources and databases that I use in my work.

Exoplanet Databases & Archives

    Exoplanet Databases & Archives

    • NASA Exoplanet Archive - Comprehensive database of confirmed and potential exoplanets and their properties
    • TESS Data Portal - Access to data from the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite mission
    • JWST Science Data - Access James Webb Space Telescope data used for exoplanet characterization
    • MAST Portal - Mikulski Archive for Space Telescopes providing access to multiple space mission datasets

Research Tools

  • TESSWCS - A package for adjusting World Coordinate System (WCS) of astronomical images
  • Astroquery - A set of tools for querying astronomical web services
  • Astropy - Community-developed Python library for astronomy
